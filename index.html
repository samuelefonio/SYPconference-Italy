Conference Overview

As Europe confronts a rapidly deteriorating security landscape, debates over the future of European security, strategic autonomy, and evolving risks are becoming increasingly urgent. In this context, the Student Young Pugwash (SYP) network aims to foster an informed, forward-looking, and policy-relevant dialogue on the contemporary security challenges that will shape Europe's future, placing the next generation of European decision-makers at the centre of the conversation. A pan-European Organising Committee was established in spring 2026 to organise the inaugural European SYP Conference (EuSYPC), to be held in Pisa, Italy, from 25-27 September 2026. Composed of 15-20 young professionals, the Committee includes members of UNIPI, USPID, SYP Italy, SYP UK, SYP Germany, SYP France, SYP Spain, and International SYP, embodying a collaborative effort across the leading European branches of the SYP network.

Conference Details

The conference will be held in Pisa, Italy, from 25-27 September 2026. The conference will feature a series of plenary sessions, panel discussions, and working groups, providing a platform for participants to engage with experts in the field of peace and security. The conference is free but registration is mandatory to attend. Please register for the conference by visiting the Registration page.

About Student Young Pugwash (SYP)

SYP is an international network of students and young professionals who are committed to promoting peace, security, and social justice. The organization provides a platform for young people to engage in dialogue, research, and advocacy on issues related to peace and security, with a focus on the ethical implications of science and technology.
